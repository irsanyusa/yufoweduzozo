Gleb Savchenko Fuels Rylee Arnold, Harry Jowsey Dating Rumors on 'DWTS'

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Photos by: Disney/Eric McCandless

Reading Time: 2 minutes

During Dancing With The Stars’ exciting semifinal episode, Gleb Savchenko added another layer of curiosity to the ongoing romance rumors between Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold. Savchenko apparently couldn’t resist from fueling the rumors, since he pointed out the bracelet Jowsey recently gifted to Arnold.

Gleb Savchenko Made a Subtle Reference to Rylee Arnold’s Bracelet

On Tuesday night, Savchenko decided to stir the pot. He strategically drew attention to a bracelet on 18-year-old Arnold’s wrist – the same $15,000 Van Cleef & Arpels jewelry gifted to her by 26-year-old Jowsey after their elimination.

As contestants Ariana Madix and Pasha Pashkov received their scores following a jive routine, the camera panned to Arnold smiling and shaking her head in the background. Savchenko stood next to her with an arm wrapped around her shoulders. He then lifted her arm up and shook the gold bracelet to put it on full display.

The gesture seemed intentional, to fuel rumors that Arnold and Jowsey are more than dance partners and friends. Speculation around a possible off-screen romance has followed the young duo since DWTS premiered in September.

If you missed it, Jowsey surprised Arnold with the expensive Van Cleef & Arpels bracelet shortly after their DWTS elimination. She showcased the gift in a Thanksgiving Day TikTok video.

“I think it is literally the most beautiful bracelet I’ve ever seen. I’m the luckiest girl ever. Wow. It’s so cute.” Arnold said.

The Australian reality star also spent the holiday with Arnold’s family in Los Angeles after she invited him so he wouldn’t be alone for the occasion.

Rylee Arnold, Harry Jowsey Deny Dating Rumors

Both Arnold and Jowsey have shot down speculation that their close partnership has turned into a real-life romance. Jowsey called the dating rumors “unfair” in an October interview, clarifying that DWTS is “not a dating show, it’s a dancing show.”

Arnold echoed similar sentiments about wanting to focus on their choreography and performances rather than relationship status. However, their undeniable chemistry and flirty interactions have kept dating speculation alive.

Savchenko’s subtle jewelry move is the latest instance of DWTS personalities appearing to fuel Arnold and Jowsey dating rumors instead of quelling them. While Arnold and Jowsey’s off-screen relationship remains ambiguous, Savchenko and others seem intent on keeping the romance rumor fire burning.
