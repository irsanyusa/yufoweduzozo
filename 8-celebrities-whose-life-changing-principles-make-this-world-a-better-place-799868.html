8 Celebrities Whose Life-Changing Principles Make This World a Better Place

Actors work for about 12 to 20 hours a day, on average. Being a famous singer equals long roads, packed concerts, and hardly any privacy. But, despite their extremely busy schedule and difficulties, there are some celebrities who are determined to use their platform to make a positive impact on the world.

In our article below, Bright Side has featured 8 of these personalities who are not just famous for their skills.

1. Benedict Cumberbatch is bringing equality to Hollywood.

The concept of gender and racial inequality is nothing new to Hollywood, but at least there is someone who has decided to not just speak up but to actually do something about it. Famous for his roles in Doctor Strange and Sherlock, Benedict Cumberbatch has refused to work in projects where his female counterpart will not receive equal pay.

2. Keanu Reeves is secretly funding hospitals.

Keanu Reeves is the internet’s favorite man in the world and there’s a reason for it. In addition to funding cancer hospitals and secretly helping with the treatment of sick children, Keanu never hesitates to give back to the world. According to him, money is the last thing on his mind and he often donates a part of his salary for the good.

3. Megan Fox is raising her children without gender barriers.

Megan Fox is a believer that kids shouldn’t fit into the gender roles that society has decided for them. She lets her son wear pink and even wear dresses to school. She admits that his looks raise some eyebrows, but she doesn’t care since she’s trying to make her children more confident.

4. Ricky Gervais is working for animal welfare.

Not only does Ricky Gervais constantly work for the betterment of animals, but he has also revealed that he will leave his whole fortune to animal charities. He also never misses a chance to call out Hollywood for its hypocrisy and inequality in his own, humorous way.

5. Nicki Minaj is helping out an Indian village.

Everyone knows of this rapper’s music but not many are aware of her philanthropic works. Nicki has been donating money for years to rebuild a village in India. She’s constructed wells to fight the problem of unsafe drinking water, opened a computer center, and facilitated their access to education. Nicki also paid several of her fans’ tuition fees when they score straight As and show her.

6. Kesha officiates LGBTQ+ weddings.

The Tik Tok singer who is openly bisexual is doing her part to stand up for the LGBTQ community. She often portrays characters of different sexual orientations in her music videos and has helped in officiating weddings for people in that community.

7. Ashton Kutcher is fighting against human trafficking.

Ashton Kutcher has been working actively to end human trafficking and has already saved thousands of people. His organization Thorn, which he co-founded with his ex-wife Demi Moore, uses the technology and resources of Google, Yahoo, Microsoft, and Facebook, to help the government end the problem of slavery and trafficking.

8. Dolly Parton is educating needy children.

Through her foundation that is funded by her profits from her work, Dolly Parton provides jobs to the needy. She also sends a book per month to every enrolled child, to increase literacy. Currently, 850,000 children across the globe are a part of this.

If you were a celebrity with a huge platform, what would be the first thing you’d change about the world?
