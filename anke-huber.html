Anke Huber (Tennis Player) - On This Day

Profession: Tennis Player

Biography: Anke Huber is a German tennis player, best known as runner-up in women's singles at the 1995 WTA Finals and the 1996 Australian Open. She has won twelve singles titles and one doubles title on the WTA, as well as finishing within the top twenty for ten seasons.
