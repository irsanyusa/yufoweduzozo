Whoopi Goldberg Is Now Making 'Ugly' Christmas Sweaters

October 12, 2016 1:49 PM EDT

It’s never too early to start preparing for the holidays. And with Whoopi Goldberg’s fun new festive sweater collection, dressing up for those ugly sweater parties will be a snap this season.

The View host, marijuana entrepreneur, and quirky comedian partnered with Lord & Taylor to produce a line of, well, very unusual holiday-themed sweaters—but don’t call them “ugly,” Goldberg warns.

“Mine are kind of ‘funny Christmas sweaters’ or ‘Christmas sweaters with a twist,'” she explained to Vogue. The kitschy tongue-in-cheek designs are both comic and socially conscious; there are reindeers as dancing Rockettes on one, a turtleneck depicting a happily mixed family on another, and a Hanukkah-themed sweater featuring an octopus as an eight-armed menorah.

“It’s basically about how we are all in this together… It’s all in my funny, weird head of things that I like to see. There’s a great one of two Santas kissing, one white Santa and one black Santa, and it’s fantastic. It’s anything that will elicit a smile or an ‘aw’ from you; that’s what I want,” she said.

Each of the 11 unisex sweaters has a name (“Bite Me” has a gingerbread man taking a taste of his fellow, for instance), WWD reports, and are priced at $139. They go on sale Nov. 1. Goldberg told Vogue will be both a Kwanzaa and Ramadan sweater next year. And this could be just the start of Goldberg’s fashion forays; one peek at her Instagram reveals her pre-existing penchant for wacky footwear.
