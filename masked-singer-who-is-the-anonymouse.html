We’ve heard some wild names before on The Masked Singer, from the Thingamajig to the Whatchamacallit to the Pi-Rat. And in season 10? We have the Anonymouse, not to be confused with the Mouse, Dionne Warwick.

Anonymouse is also the ONLY Masked Singer contestant who we’ve heard sing in any preview. So who is the celebrity under the mask? Let’s get into it!

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 10

Before the Season 10 Kickoff, we got an Instagram video tease, then a preview of her performance days before the episode released. Finally, on the Kickoff episode, we got a whole slew of new clues!

RELATED: WHO’S PERFORMING ON ‘THE MASKED SINGER’ SEASON 10 PREMIERE?

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Anonymouse

Fans online heard that voice and knew “EXACTLY who this is,” as panelist Ken Jeong would say. Basically every prediction is that this is either Demi Lovato or Kelly Clarkson, and I can hear both in the performance!

Who is the Anonymouse on The Masked Singer?

Well that voice is certainly telling because it could only belong to one person. And that celebrity is…

Demi Lovato!

While we don’t have the clues to back it up, her performance from the preview says it all. And if it’s not her, we’ve definitely been baited and switched because that voice is UNCANNY!

Tune in to The Masked Singer on FOX this Sunday, September 10th, to see if we’re right and check out all the new costumes in season 10.
