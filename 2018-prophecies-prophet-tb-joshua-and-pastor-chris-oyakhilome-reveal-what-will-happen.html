Prophet T.B Joshua and Pastor Chris Oyakhilome reveal what will happen in the new year

According to the founder of the Synagogue Church of All Nations (SCOAN), 2018 will be amazing as last year went away with every unfavorable situation.

He made this prophecy at the CrossOver service at the church’s headquarters in Ikotun area of Lagos.

Prophet T.B Joshua said, “On behalf of Prophet TB Joshua and the Emmanuel TV family worldwide, we wish you all a Happy New Year!!!

ADVERTISEMENT

“Let the relationship between you and God in this New Year be reestablished in Jesus’ Name! Receive a free spirit—a spirit that is free from offense, bitterness, unforgiveness, and pain of the past. Receive it in Jesus’ name, I command!”

“Every unfavorable situation you have faced in the year 2017 has ended with the year! Every unfavorable situation in your business, career, marriage—cease, in the name of Jesus Christ!

“As you enter the New Year, whatever barrier between you and the Spirit of God—be removed, in the name of Jesus Christ! Whatever wall between you and God—be removed, in the name of Jesus Christ!”

He ended by urging Christians to embrace God in 2018. In his words, “In the New Year, you are called to God’s kind of lifestyle.

“I mean, His Divine essence in terms of faithfulness, steadfastness, and love. Love your neighbor as yourself.”

2018 Prophecy from Pastor Chris Oyakhilome

The founder of Believers’ LoveWorld aka Christ Embassy has also released his prophecies at the New Year’s Eve Service.

He started by declaring 2018 as ‘the Year of the Supernatural!’, then proceeded to make his prophecies in stages of revelation, Love World News reports.

ADVERTISEMENT

First, Pastor Chris defined ‘blessing’, by saying “to bless is to cause to prosper, to favor, to evoke successful outcomes, to infuse with divine abilities.” Members were instructed to memorize this.

He went on to announce that this year’s blessing will be seen in seven areas—supernatural supply; supernatural strength; supernatural abilities; supernatural insight; supernatural wisdom; supernatural knowledge; and, supernatural health!

The man of God added,  “There’s an intervention from the Heavens; angels are ministering, and the Lord Himself is taking center stage! You’re going to see the unexpected. You’re about to see the unimaginable.”
