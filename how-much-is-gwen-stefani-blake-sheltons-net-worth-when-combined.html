How Much is Gwen Stefani, Blake Shelton's Net Worth When Combined?

Before Gwen Stefani and Blake Shelton’s romance began on The Voice, they had both already created a name for themselves in Hollywood. Thus, as husband and wife, it’s not surprising to know that their net worth combined is insane. Let’s find out how much the power couple is worth.

Gwen Stefani, Blake Shelton’s Net Worth is a Whopping $260 Million When Combined

Stefani tasted early success during the ’90s as the lead vocalist of ska punk band, No Doubt. Introducing their alternative rock sound to the world, their 1995 album Tragic Kingdom swept everyone off their feet, resulting to the sold out of its 16 million copies worldwide.

After hiatus of the band, Stefani later explored the music industry as a solo artist. Thanks to her first album, Love.Angel.Music.Baby (L.A.M.B.), she was able to sell more than seven million copies of the collection. Her next albums were successful as well, further contributing to her monetary sweep.

Over the years, Stefani worked with countless artists, launched a perfume and fashion line, served as ambassadress to various brands, seized a Las Vegas residency, and assumed a coaching role on The Voice. Adding all her income streams, Stefani earned herself a net worth of $140 million, according to celebritynetworth.com.

Meanwhile, Shelton kicked off his country music career in the early ’00s, releasing “Austin” as his debut single. In 2001, he dropped his debut album, spawning several hits like “Ol’ Red.” He later released nine more albums, once more introducing number one hits. By now, he has sold over 10 million albums worldwide.

It pays noting as well that much of the country crooner’s earnings come from his involvement on The Voice. Nonetheless, Shelton also has a bunch of brand endorsements — Pizza Hut, Pepsi, Land’s End, Gildan, Smithworks Vodka and Walmart, to name a few. Adding the coins he receive during tours as well as his earnings from his game show Barmageddon, Shelton is worth $120 million.

When combined, Stefani and Shelton are worth a total of $260 million. Insane, we know.

Shakira Remains to be The Voice Coach with the Highest Net Worth

The Voice couple may be worth that massive amount, but neither of them are the coach with the highest net worth. Shakira, who served as a coach during seasons four and six, the richest coach by far.

The Colombian superstar started her career at the age of 13 in the early ’90s. Her first English-language album Laundry Service allowed her to be known all over the globe, selling nearly 100 million records worldwide. Some of her worthy ventures include dance, cosmetics, and philanthropy. Overall, Shakira has insane $300 million net worth.

The Voice season 23 coaching panel consists of Stefani, Niall Horan, Reba McEntire, and John Legend. These Hollywood stars are also worth millions, caused by their notable contribution in the musical arena.

Horan’s net worth, since he joined boy band One Direction, stands at $70 million. The Queen of Country, on the other hand, boasts a net worth of $95 million, reaping the success of her musicality and several other businesses. Lastly, Legend, who began as other artists’ pianist, now has a net worth of $100 million.
