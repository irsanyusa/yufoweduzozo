Season 3 gets premiere date, adds Loretta Devine and Jill Scott as guest stars [Video]

The season has also added 'Grey Anatomy' actress Loretta Devine and singer-actress Jill Scott as guest stars.

Devine will play Cecilia, a bookstore owner whose car was hit by Mary Jane’s vehicle in the season 2 finale. She would also try to exhort $25,000 from Mary Jane.

According to BET, the two would bond somewhere along the line.

Scott will play Jackie, Niecy’s (Raven Goodwin) mom, who according to BET, is “back in town just long enough to show her [daughter] some tough love and Patrick some much needed sexual attention.”

According to showrunner Mara Brock Akil “After receiving that phone call from David and getting into a car accident, Mary Jane has no other options but to work on her recovery from the accident, from toxic relationships, and from her self-destructive ways.

It’s a season that will remind fans why they fell in love with Mary Jane to begin with. She’s flawed and somewhat broken, but watching her piece her life together is beautiful.”
