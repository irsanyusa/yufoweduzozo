Why Older Women Tend to Fall for Younger Men

Today, more and more women are choosing to date younger men. Celebs like Kate Moss, Priyanka Chopra, and Heidi Klum don’t see age as a barrier when it comes to love, so why should we? In the past, dating younger men was less accepted by society and frowned-upon — but we think that things have changed. Women can choose who they date and nothing will stand in their way.

Bright Side has found some of the reasons why women go for beaus younger than they are, and we’re learning what we may be missing out on.

Younger men smell more attractive to women.

This is because their bodies are at the peak of their health. In fact, a study shows that the older we get, the stronger we smell as our body odor changes. Young men are also more likely to wear scents like cologne and body spray to stay sharp. It’s worth noting that smells are a huge part of attraction as we unconsciously fall for their odor, as well as everything else.

Younger men experience the world differently.

Because they’re part of another generation, they may do things completely differently. This can open the minds of their partner as they show them how things are done today, introducing them to a whole new world. This can be fun for both of them as they get to share their 2 worlds together.

Younger men are more stylish.

Youthful guys are generally known to pay more attention to how they look and dress. For example, they may choose style over comfort and keep up with the latest trends. Actually, according to one study, how men dress is an important part of what women find attractive about them since they find the way a person presents themselves to be crucial.

Younger men are full of energy.

This is an inevitable factor because this energy can add new experiences to the relationship. Youthful boyfriends may be more eager to try new things and stay on the move, which can mean experiencing unique activities together that you wouldn’t normally do. This may help to keep the relationship exciting and full of adventures, adding more memories to their photo album.

Younger men can be more flattering.

Unfortunately, there is a lot of competition between women, and when young guys go for older women, it can often be celebrated. In today’s society, women who are past the age of 30 are considered “old” by many people, especially if they’re single. So when a younger man dates an older woman, he is ignoring these stigmas and showing her the appreciation she deserves instead.

Younger men don’t (necessarily) want children yet.

As couples get older, they may feel pressure to start settling down. However, guys with fewer numbers on the clock are probably still a long way off from the point of wanting kids, which can be great if the woman doesn’t want children. One study shows us that men are delaying having children more in recent years, many choosing to have them when they’re in their thirties and later.

Younger men have more time for you.

Divorce, career, and disappointments — dating someone younger generally means you can skip such baggage. It’s more likely that younger guys aren’t tied-down with such concerns. This can be perfect for having more time together. This means that he’s not dividing his time so much between the woman and other major obligations in his life, such as children.

Younger men are more fertile.

It can work well if you plan on having children together because the younger the man, the more fertile he likely is. Problems with conception can be stressful and often put a strain on any relationship, which may be escapable with a younger partner.
