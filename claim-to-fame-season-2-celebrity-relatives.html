Claim to Fame is back for season two! And if you aren’t already a fan of this celebrity guessing game, buckle up because this show is quite the ride. Celebrity relatives of some HUGE names compete for a $100,000 grand prize, fighting to hide their identities and guess everyone else’s celebrity connections.

Even episode one brought an iconic moment of reality TV when Tom Hanks’ niece, Carly Reeves, melted down after being eliminated.

Now let’s get into who everyone else is related to!

Two Truths and a Lie: Uncle (lie), Musician, Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame

Celebrity Relative: Donny Osmond’s son

Who is Cole related to?

Two Truths and a Lie: Father (lie), singer, Grammy Award

Celebrity Relative: Alicia Keys’ brother

Who is Gabriel related to on Claim to Fame?

Two Truths and a Lie: Brother, athlete (lie), NAACP Image Award

Celebrity Relative: Nick Cannon’s brother

Who is Hugo related to on Season 2?

Two Truths and a Lie: Grandfather, athlete (lie), Nobel Peace Prize

Celebrity Relative: Jimmy Carter’s grandson

Who is Jane related to on Claim to Fame Season 2?

Two Truths and a Lie: Father (lie), musician, Grammy Award

Celebrity Relative: Dolly Parton’s niece

Who is J.R. related to?

Two Truths and a Lie: Brother, singer, NBA Championship Ring (lie)

Celebrity Relative: Lil Nas X’s brother

Who is Karsyn related to on Claim to Fame?

Two Truths and a Lie: Uncle, musician (lie), Hall of Fame

Celebrity Relative: Dale Earnhardt Jr.’s niece and Dale Earnhardt Sr.’s granddaughter

Who is Monay related to on Season 2?

Two Truths and a Lie: Father, athlete (lie), Emmy Award

Celebrity Relative: JB Smoove’s daughter

Who is Olivia related to on Claim to Fame Season 2?

Two Truths and a Lie: Brother (lie), Razzie Award

Celebrity Relative: Jenny McCarthy-Wahlberg’s niece

Who is Shayne related to?

Two Truths and a Lie: Father, musician (lie), Grammy Award

Clue: The wine bottle clue for Shayne had a crown, face with a question mark, destination pin and plane, a map of the US, an ampersand, a play button, a deer with antlers, and wheat. Decoding this, the first half is a reference to the movie Coming to America and the second half reads “plays Buckwheat” (a famous character on SNL).

Celebrity Relative: Eddie Murphy’s daughter

Who is Travis related to on Claim to Fame?

Two Truths and a Lie: Father, actor (lie), Critics’ Choice Award

Celebrity Relative: Neil deGrasse Tyson’s son

And that’s all the celebrity relative reveals for season 2 of Claim to Fame! If you want to watch more all season, check me out on TikTok!
